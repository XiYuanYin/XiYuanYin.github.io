I'm a postdoc at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in the Sciences (MiS) in Leipzig, where I work in the Applied Analysis group under the mentorship of Prof. László Székelyhidi.

I obtained my PhD in Mathematics at McGill University under the supervision of Prof. Jean-Christophe Nave. I then worked as postdoc at the Fluid Mechanics and Acoustics Laboratory (LMFA) in Lyon under the mentorship of Prof. Wouter Bos.


Research Interests

Most of my work so far is centered around the study of numerical methods for advection dominated problems. While computational fluid mechanics was the principal focus of my reseach, I also have a strong interest for the relevant underlying mathematical theories:

  • Numerical analysis
  • Analysis of partial differential equations
  • Differential geometry (in particular, Geometric Hydrodynamics)
